## Wesley - Diretor Musical

**Weekly rhythm:**
- Monday: Banda Roxa rehearsal, schools (Pereira Passos, Estados Unidos) - heaviest day
- Tuesday: Leadership meeting (~1h30)
- Wednesday: Banda Preta rehearsal, tuba lesson
- Thursday: No fixed activities
- Friday: Banda Preta rehearsal, Pedagogy meeting (~1h30)

**Key processes:**

### Band Management
- Directs Bandas Preta and Roxa (main bands)
- Repertoire management: Physical folders with students, planning Coda spreadsheet
- Band hierarchy: Preta (Wesley) > Roxa (Wesley) > Verde (Shanso) > Amarela (Raffaella)
- Constant communication between conductors

### Exam & Promotion System
- Theory exams: 2x/year (end of semester), designed by Tom, applied by Wesley
- Practical exams: 1-2x/year via Music Teachers Board (UK)
- Promotions: End of semester only, based on exam results + vacancy needs
- Notification flow: Wesley notifies teachers/parents/students, Iris sends official communication

### Performance Management
- Requests come via Tom
- Wesley + Tom decide which band performs
- Iris handles parent authorizations
- Wesley conducts all shows (~15/year)

### Instrument Maintenance
- Teachers report broken instruments
- Wesley provides replacement from stock
- Instrument goes to luthier (Guidolini for brass, Davide for percussion)
- Tom approves repair budget
- Wesley swaps back with student

### Teacher Coordination
- 11 teachers supervised
- Hiring: Get recommendations, verify CV, Wesley + Tom decide together
- Class division: By instrument + level, stays fixed for year
- Teacher meetings: 2x/year (end of semester)

**Dependencies:**
- On Tom: Budget approvals, strategic decisions, theory exam design
- On Iris: Communications, reports, occurrence records
- On Lillian: Social work escalations
- On teachers: Broken instrument reports

## Lillian - Assistente Social

**Weekly rhythm:**
- Works in two social projects simultaneously
- FB hours mainly in evening (class time)
- Daytime: Interviews with parents as schedules align
- Fixed: Friday morning team meeting (1h30)

**Key processes:**

### Initial Social Interview
- Trigger: Iris sends new student contact
- Schedule interview (in-person or video call)
- Cover: Household size, housing conditions, family income, relationships, student routine/health
- Record in physical notebook
- If relevant to project: Share with Wesley and Tom (maintaining professional confidentiality)
- Signal to team if observation needed

### External Contacts & Guidance
- Situations: Custody issues, child support, school conflicts, distant school placement
- Contact appropriate body (Conselho Tutelar, Vara da Infância, schools) OR
- Guide parent on how to seek help
- Track based on agency responses and family adherence

### Individual Student Conversations
- Trigger: Parent request or own observation
- Situations: Inappropriate behavior, not wanting to study, excessive phone use, rude responses
- Warn student beforehand, use class day break time
- Track changes via: Own observation, teacher feedback, parent feedback

### Travel Authorization (Vara da Infância)
- Trigger: Trip confirmed in team meeting
- Contact Vara da Infância to signal trip
- Vara starts process
- Parents go in person to sign
- Iris tracks execution, signals issues needing intervention
- Lead time: 1 month or ASAP after trip confirmed

**Tools:** Physical notebook (wants to digitize), paper student list

**Pain points:**
- Waiting for parent responses (main bottleneck)
- Waiting for public agency responses
- Need for private space for in-person meetings
