## Wesley - Musical Director

**Official Role:** Diretor Musical
**Salary:** R$7.379/mês + extras (events >40, travel days >12, exam grading)

**What He Actually Does:**
- Supervises all teachers day-to-day
- Runs bands and rehearsals
- Manages routine discipline and student-facing issues
- Last line of defense between Tom and parents (massive win)
- Handles instrument logistics, repairs, inventory, physical spaces
- Runs weekly pedagogy/program meetings
- On-site manager at events
- Represents Favela Brass in the field

**Strengths:**
- Reliability dialed to 11 - he will always do what needs doing
- Absolute loyalty - fully identifies with the project
- Strong musician, good enough as MD
- Excellent with people - especially tricky parents, teachers, students
- Handles conflict & hard conversations without fear
- Operational continuity - fills gaps, fixes fires
- Genuine vocation - not just a job, he lives FB

**Weaknesses / Risks:**
- No natural business/admin/strategic skillset (spreadsheets, finance, governance, marketing, planning - not his lane)
- Impulse-driven - wants to fix everything immediately, creates budget turbulence
- Has favorites / inconsistent fairness (common in MDs, needs management)
- Not process-oriented - great at action, not systems
- Still needs "Tom the stabilizer" in big decisions

**Growth Trajectory:**
- Wants to grow as MD, not move into exec/ops/business roles
- No appetite for governance, strategy, admin
- Long-term stable pillar - will not "become Tom" (nor should he)

**Organizational Function:**
He is the COO of the physical world, but NOT the COO of the organizational world.

Perfect head of: Teaching, Students, Staff discipline, On-site operations, Rehearsals, Events logistics, Day-to-day problem solving, Parent interface

NOT the head of: Strategy, Governance, Finance/admin, Fundraising, Communications, Systems, Organizational growth
